Guardians of the Galaxy
Infinity Stones Edition

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

The Infinity Stones are below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game and save the Universe by matching your total score to the random number; you lose the game and Thanos will create the Infinity Gauntlet if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each stone is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of the stones will change.
